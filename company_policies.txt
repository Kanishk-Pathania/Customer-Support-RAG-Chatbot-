# COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENTS

## 1. REFUND POLICY
Effective Date: January 2026

Standard Refund Window: Customers may request a full refund for products within 30 days of the original purchase date.
Conditions for Refund: Items must be in their original packaging, unused, and accompanied by the original receipt.
Exceptions: Perishable goods, customized items, and digital software downloads are non-refundable once accessed or opened.
Processing Time: Approved refunds will be credited back to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.

---

## 2. CANCELLATION POLICY
Effective Date: January 2026

Service Subscriptions: Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time; however, access will continue until the end of the current billing cycle.
Appointment Cancellation: We require a minimum of 24 hours' notice for cancelling service appointments. 
Late Fees: Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice will incur a cancellation fee of 50% of the service cost.
No-Shows: Failure to arrive for an appointment without prior notice will result in a 100% charge of the service fee.

---

## 3. SHIPPING POLICY
Effective Date: January 2026

Domestic Shipping: We offer standard shipping (5–7 business days) and expedited shipping (2–3 business days).
International Shipping: Delivery times vary by region but generally range from 10 to 21 business days.
Shipping Costs: Orders over $50 qualify for free standard domestic shipping. For orders under $50, a flat rate of $5.99 applies.
Tracking: Once an order is dispatched, a tracking number will be sent via email within 24 hours.
